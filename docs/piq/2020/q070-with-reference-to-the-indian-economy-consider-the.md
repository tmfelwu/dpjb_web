<ol>
<li>“Commercial Paper’ is a short-term unsecured promissory note.</li>
<li>‘Certificate of Deposit’ is a long term instrument issued by the Reserve Bank of India to a corporation.</li>
<li>‘Call Money’ is a short-term finance used for interbank transactions.</li>
<li>‘Zero-Coupon Bonds’ are the interest bearing short-term bonds issued by the Scheduled Commercial Banks to corporations.</li>
</ol>
