        <p>Consider the following statements: <br />
Statement-I: India's public sector health care system largely focuses on curative care with limited preventive, promotive and rehabilitative care. <br />
Statement-II: Under India's decentralized approach to health care delivery, the States are primarily responsible for organizing health services. <br />
