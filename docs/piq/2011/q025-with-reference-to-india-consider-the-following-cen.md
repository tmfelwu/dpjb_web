        <p>With reference to India, consider the following Central Acts:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Import and Export (Control) Act, 1947.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mining and Mineral Development (Regulation) Act, 1957</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Customs Act, 1962</p>
</li>
<li>

</li>
