        <p>With reference to food chains in ecosystems, consider the following statements:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>A food chain illustrates the order in which a chain of organism feed upon each other.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Food chains are found within the populations of a species.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A food chain illustrates the numbers of each organism which are eaten by others.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Which of the statement given above is/are correct?</p>
